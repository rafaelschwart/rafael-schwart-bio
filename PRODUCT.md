# PRODUCT.md

> Written for the impeccable skill — concise brand brief that survives sessions.

**Register:** brand. The site IS the product. It exists to convince a recruiter, hiring manager, or co-founder to take Rafael seriously in 90 seconds.

## Product

A single-page personal portfolio at https://rafaelschwart.com for **Rafael Schwart**, a PMP-certified Senior Operations Program Manager (NPI) at Motorola Solutions in Miami, FL. 10+ years across consumer electronics, AR (Magic Leap), medical devices (Stryker robotics), aerospace, automation, and solar. Bilingual EN/ES, born Caracas, Venezuela.

## Users

1. **Recruiters / hiring managers** for senior program management, NPI, and process engineering roles. They want a fast scan: title, employer, dates, certs, hard outcomes.
2. **Background-check vendors** verifying employment dates and titles. They want official letters of verification on letterhead.
3. **AI agents** (ChatGPT, Claude, Perplexity) crawling the site for someone asking "who is Rafael Schwart?" — they want llms.txt and per-section deep links.
4. **Co-founders / investors** evaluating Rafael's side ventures (Tiento Labs, Earth Robotics, Recovrz). They want to see range.

## Voice

Confident, declarative, technical. Sentence fragments allowed in headlines ("Engineering programs, end-to-end."). Em dashes are part of Rafael's voice — preserve them in bio copy. No emoji. No marketing hype words ("revolutionary," "game-changing"). Numbers do persuasion ("15% production lift at Magic Leap," "50%+ test cycle reduction at Gables").

## Anti-references

- **No SaaS-cream "AI workflow" landing pages** — purple gradients, Inter everything, 3-column feature cards, hero metric template.
- **No corporate boilerplate stock photography** — manufacturing line + project management + engineering workspace stock photos were removed in the 2026-05-01 redesign and should not return.
- **No Apple-clone single-blue-accent commerce site** — the Apple system was the design starting point but the user explicitly redirected to a warmer, more humanist Lovable language.
- **No dark editorial portfolio** with serif-on-black + neon accent. The site is light, warm, paper-toned.
- **No sidebar with section navigation** — retired in the 2026-05-01 redesign in favor of single-page scroll with sticky top nav.

## Strategic principles

1. **Real evidence over polish.** Rafael's strongest signal is brand logos (Motorola, Magic Leap, Stryker) + verification letters + named recommenders + measured outcomes. The design exists to display these, not decorate around them.
2. **Single-page scroll, URL-deep-linkable.** AI agents and recruiters share links — `/experience`, `/contact` must still resolve and scroll to the right anchor. The router preserves crawlability while the UX is one continuous page.
3. **Em dash voice stays.** Rafael's bio copy uses em dashes deliberately. New UI strings I write avoid them per impeccable; user-authored copy is voice and is preserved.
4. **The headshot is the photograph.** One B&W professional portrait, used at 1:1 in the hero. No additional photography. The bio sits on warm paper, not over imagery.
